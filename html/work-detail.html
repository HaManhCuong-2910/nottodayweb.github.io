ABOUT

FRANKENSTEIN

The leitmotif of the collection is the assemblage of eclectic puzzle pieces with the intention of recreating normalcy. However, as with the monster created in Mary Shelley’s Frankenstein, something uncanny is pieced together in instead, “When I looked around I saw and heard of none like me. Was I, then, a monster…?” By no means. My “monster” is not only aware of his unconventional and unbecoming appearance but assumes it with resplendence and self-assurance.
