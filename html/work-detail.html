FRANKENSTEIN PROJECT

The leitmotif of the collection is the tragic context of trying puzzle pieces together with the intention of a normal thing; but ended up with something uncanny. Like the monster in Frankenstein, ”I looked around I saw and heard of none like me. Was I, then, a monster.” My Frankenstein is aware of his unconventional and unfitting appearance and can only react with it by acceptance.
